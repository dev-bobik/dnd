Races & Lineages

A visit to one of the great cities in the worlds of Dungeons & Dragons overwhelms the senses. Voices chatter in countless different languages. Buildings in myriad architectural styles display the diverse origins of their inhabitants. And the people themselves represent many different races, from diminutive halflings and stout dwarves to majestically beautiful elves.
